FEATURES & BENEFITS

Patient Portals, Remote Monitoring, Data Visualization & Patient Intervention Management distributed through Software Development Kits and Web access tools.

SUPPORTING CLINICAL METHODS

VISAV supports evidence-based, patient-centric decisions by creating networks that provide hard data with subjective preferences that have the potential to enable patients and healthcare providers to carry out and implement care more effectively in any setting.

INSTANT ACCESS TO PATIENT ACTIVITY

VISAV collects, tracks and monitors patient-generated information, making it accessible to patients, providers, and authorized family members at any time. Clear, easy-to-understand charts show patient progress, compliance and engagement.

COMPREHENSIVE PROGRESS REPORTS

VISAV provides patient-generated reports that present a broader view of patient activity, compliance and behaviour. Third-party health and statistical data stores can be connected for additional supportive information relative to symptoms and behaviour.

CARE PROVIDER NOTIFICATIONS

VISAV monitors patient behaviour and presents trend and outlier data to healthcare teams to make therapeutic plan decisions. Notifications are coordinated across a range of channels (E-mail, SMS, Remote Push Notifications, APIs), ensuring clear, effective communication, to notify the right people at the right time.

INTEGRATED HEALTH SERVICES

VISAV provides a range of additional Telehealth services, to aid in patient communication and physician reimbursement. This includes patient scheduling, video conferencing, digital prescription services, and remote patient monitoring visualization and management tools.

FLEXIBLE DEVELOPMENT TOOLS

VISAV Telehealth services are fully integrable and Software Development Kits can be used across any connected platform or device. For web services, an API explorer provides detailed REST access points with a testing interface to make integration as easy as possible.

We make it easier for physicians to assess a patient's knowledge and understanding of each treatment, by tracking and monitoring behaviour and providing tools that support intervention. We believe that a more connected Healthcare network will reduce care costs and empower patients and their families as active players in the management and treatment of their condition.
